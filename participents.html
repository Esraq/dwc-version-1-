of the conference.

It is expected that 29 countries from South Asia,South East Asia,East Asia and Asia-Pacific shall participate in the conference.A high levele team of at least three members from each country is expected to attend the conference. The team should ideally consisit of the Minister of the relevant ministry,a high level government official involved in policy making a water sector experts from universities of knowledge based institutes.It is expected that all of themm are familier with SDG6.The participating countries are expected to share their status on water and sanitation,express their views and plan to achieve water related SDG targets and highlight the challenges and possible way foroward through a country paper. Apart from Government delegates,some representatives of development partners and international NGOs will be invited to participate.Each of that country will nominate one participant who will participate in the preparation of strategy and Dhaka Declaration.
