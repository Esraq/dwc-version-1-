Welcome to Dhaka Water Conference 2017

Description
of the conference.

The Hono'rable Prime Minister(PM) Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh is one of the members of HLPW.It is the commitment of the PM of Bangladesh to hold a national workshop and a international conference . on water towards achieving targets related to water and particularly .Sustainable Development Goal 6.The national workshop has alraedy been held in November 2016 in Dhaka.An international water conference as Dhaka Water Conference,has now been planned to be held in July 2017.

Mainly three events will take place in these conference.These are water conference,Delta working group conference and HLPW Sherpa meeting.

Dhaka water conference will take place from 27th to 31st july.Various events and discussion will take place here and matter related to water will be presented and sollution of problem related to water will be discoverd. Delta Working group meeting will take place in 28 July 2017.Only invited participents will take place in these meeting and will give their important opinions on certain topics. Sherpa meeting will also take place in these conference parallel to the Dhaka water conference

The regional water conference will act as a platform for comprehensive discussion on water related issue focusing the SDG6.The participating countries will share their status on water and sanitation express their views plane to achieve SDG targets and highlight the challenges and possible way forward in the conference. The conference will also provide an opportunity to discuss among the participants about gaps,challenges and expense learned during the implementation of Millenium Development Goals(MDG). It is expected that the conference participants will generate recommendations and the proposals for further measures to be taken at national,regional and international levels to achieve the regional water-related targets focusing the sustainable development goals especially SDG 6 and relevant sub sections. The conference will also promote awareness among the participating countries towards ensuring sustainable management of water and sanitation for all level of the community involving the government and non-government organizations as other stakeholders at all levels involved in the water sector.
